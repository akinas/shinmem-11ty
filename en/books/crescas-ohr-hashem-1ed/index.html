Light of the Lord

Rabbi Hasdai Crescas

Edition year: 2019

Read More

The inaugural publication of Hasdai Crescas’s Light of the Lord, based on the earliest manuscript.

This first edition marked the beginning of a new textual path for Or Hashem, offering a departure from flawed print traditions. Though later editions introduced minor corrections, the first edition remains the foundation of this textual project.

  • 200 copies printed
  • 400 pages
  • Dimensions: 145 x 225 mm
